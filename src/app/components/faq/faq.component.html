How much does a steak really cost?

Most shoppers think it's the cost per pound, but they're wrong according to the U.S. Department of Agriculture. The key word here is "Eat". The real cost of beef is the cost per serving. The fat and bone you trim off before cooking or leave on your plate is part of the total beef cost, but the indicator of eating value per dollar is the cost per edible portion.
